Athletic History

My athletic journey began in elementary school wen my Dad trained me to run a 5k and took me to Florida to compete. Ever since then I participated in the Cross country and Track & Field teams from Middle School through High School. There pursuits went well for me in the later stage when I quit Cross Country and began taking the Decathlon seriously. I placed my achievements online and with the help of the ACT score I was accepted into SAU as a student athlete. Over the course of the following 5 years I would not only become the captain of the Multi division of the team, but sucessfully broke the program record on 3 different occasions.

Academic History

As stated in the "Athletic History" section I moved to Southern Arkansas to attend University following highschool. I earned my Bachelors in computer science with a focus in cyber security in 4 years. Upon completeing my undergrad I decided to return to pursue a Masters degree in computer and information sciences with a focus in information privacy. I completed the Masters program in a single year.
